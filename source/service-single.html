Design and Build

At SAEC, we specialize in delivering complete Design & Build solutions for Low and Medium Voltage electrical systems. Our process begins with a deep understanding of your project requirements, followed by detailed engineering design using the latest CAD tools and compliance with ANSI, NEMA, and WAPDA standards.

With fully equipped in-house facilities—ranging from CNC laser cutting and powder coating to MV/LV testing labs—we fabricate high-quality, custom-built panels that are both reliable and scalable. From initial concept to final installation, SAEC ensures seamless integration, performance, and long-term support — all under one roof.

What Makes Us Different

What sets SAEC apart is our unwavering commitment to engineering excellence, certified quality, and customer satisfaction. With over 30 years of experience, we combine advanced in-house manufacturing capabilities—including CNC laser cutting and MV/LV testing labs—with a team of expert engineers to deliver WAPDA-approved, ISO-certified switchgear solutions tailored to diverse industries. Our proven track record with clients like Nestlé, Honda, and Centaurus Mall reflects our ability to deliver high-performance, custom electrical systems—on time, every time—backed by responsive after-sales support and a customer-first approach
