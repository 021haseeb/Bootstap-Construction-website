About Us

Driven by Quality, Defined by Results

South Asian Electric Company (SAEC), established in 1993, is a recognized leader in the electrical industry across Pakistan. We specialize in the design and manufacturing of high-quality switchgear panels and other electrical equipment. Built on a foundation of innovation, engineering expertise, and customer-centric values, SAEC has grown from a modest fabrication unit into a nationally acclaimed enterprise. With decades of combined experience, our management team drives strategic goals that embrace the challenges of the 21st century. We prioritize teamwork, continuous improvement, and exceptional product quality to provide our clients with unmatched value. Our aim is not only to meet industry benchmarks but to exceed them — through cutting-edge technology, efficient production methods, and attention to customer feedback. Our commitment to excellence is reflected in our certified standards (ANSI, NEMA, UL), robust quality assurance protocols, and a highly skilled workforce. SAEC proudly serves a wide array of industries including power, infrastructure, healthcare, and manufacturing. With a focus on sustainability and innovation, we continue to develop and deliver electrical solutions that power progress across Pakistan.
