Who We Are

At South Asian Electrical Concern (SAEC), we are a leading name in Pakistan’s electrical engineering landscape, known for our precision-engineered Low & Medium Voltage Switchgear Panels and reliable electrical infrastructure solutions. Since our establishment in 1993, we have consistently delivered cutting-edge products and services to industries ranging from power, textiles, pharmaceuticals, and food to telecommunication, construction, and healthcare.

"Empowering industries with precision, innovation, and unwavering commitment — we don’t just deliver panels, we deliver trust."

Whether it's design, fabrication, testing, or on-site support, we take pride in offering end-to-end services, ensuring performance, safety, and satisfaction every step of the way. At SAEC, we don’t just build panels—we power progress.
